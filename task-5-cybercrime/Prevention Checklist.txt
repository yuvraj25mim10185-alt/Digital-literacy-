Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist




- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always verify website URLs before entering details  
- Never share OTP or bank details with anyone  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly update your apps and software  
- Be cautious of messages asking for urgent action  






>>> UPI Safety Tips
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Double-check UPI ID before sending money
